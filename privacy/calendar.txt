Privacy policy

General

Simple Calendar is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using Simple Calendar, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Simple Calendar does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is used for importing events from .ics files, or storing an exported raw database containing the user events.

The `READ_CONTACTS` permission is used only in case the user wants to export contact birthdays or anniversaries.

Changes to the Policy

If the Policy changes, the modification date below will be updated. The Policy may change from time to time, so please be sure to check back periodically.
