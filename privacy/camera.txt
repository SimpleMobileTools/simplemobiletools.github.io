Privacy policy

General

Simple Camera is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using Simple Camera, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Simple Camera does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `CAMERA` permission is used for previewing and capturing photos and videos. The `RECORD_AUDIO` permission is used only during video recording, to record audio. The `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is then used to store the taken photo/video on the users' device.

Changes to the Policy

If the Policy changes, the modification date below will be updated. The Policy may change from time to time, so please be sure to check back periodically.
