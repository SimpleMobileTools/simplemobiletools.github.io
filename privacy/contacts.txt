Privacy policy

General

Simple Contacts is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using Simple Contacts, you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

Information being collected

Simple Contacts does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` permission is used for importing/exporting contacts from/to .vcf files.

The `READ_CONTACTS` and `WRITE_CONTACTS` permissions are used for accessing contact information, modifying them and creating new contacts.

Changes to the Policy

If the Policy changes, the modification date below will be updated. The Policy may change from time to time, so please be sure to check back periodically.
